Prerequisites for the Nursing Sector in UK
The candidate should meet the following requirements to work in UK.

Education and Experience

  • Nursing degree or diploma
  • One year plus of nursing experience
  • Should be registered in the nursing council of the current place of work
English Language Proficieny
  • Academic IELTS with 6.5 in writing and 7 in remaining areas
    • OR
  • OET with C+ in writing and B in remaining areas

Job Interview
 TecVive will setup an interview for the shortlisted candidates via Skype, Zoom or a similar video platform with the client.

NMC Self-Assessment
 Candidate will need to complete an online self-assessment which will confirm if he/she is eligible to apply for NMC registration. As part of this assessment, the candidate will be asked to confirm that the health, good character, and English language proficiency requirements are met.

NMC Application Assessment
 Candidates need to submit a all the necessary documents to the NMC in order to assess the suitability of the nursing role in UK. Once NMC receives all the necessary documents, the application will be reviewed by a member of the international registrations team to assess the suitability.

Computer Based Test (CBT)
 You must be registered with the Nursing & Midwifery Council (NMC). The CBT test can be taken at multiple test centers around the world.

Documents submission to the Employer
 Throughout the recruitment process, The candidates will need to submit the required documents to TecVive, which will be sent on to the employer for them to review and make sure you are legally eligible to work as a nurse in the UK. Documents required:
  • NHS application form
  • Police verification check report from the candidate’s home country
  • Documents to support the candidate’s Proof of address.
  • References

Certificate of Sponsorship (COS)
Certificate of Sponsorship (COS) is required to apply for the UK work visa. The NHS Trust/Employer acts as the candidate’s sponsor and will apply for the COS on the candidate’s behalf. The COS will last for 3 years, after which the candidate can be re-sponsored. After continuous residence of 5 years in UK, the candidate can apply for the permanent residency.

Visa Application
A Tier 2 Visa is required for the International nurses coming to work in the UK. Our clients in the U.K. will either pay or reimburse the expense to attain the visa.

Flight to the UK
 On arrival at the airport, the candidate will be picked up by a representative of our client and will be taken to your initial accommodation. Our support will be continued until you get comfortably settled.

Objective Structured Clinical Examination (OSCE)
 The second exam required for the NMC registration is the Objective Structured Clinical Examination (OSCE). The OSCE will assess the candidate’s clinical knowledge and can be completed only in the UK.

The OSCE simulates a clinical environment and patient scenarios which all registered nurses and midwives are likely to encounter when they assess, plan, implement and evaluate care.

Our clients will thoroughly prepare the candidate for the OSCE and will pay the exam fees.

Receive NMC PIN
 The candidate will receive the NMC Personal Identification Number (PIN) and will be registered with the Nursing & Midwifery Council, once the OSCE exam is passed.
